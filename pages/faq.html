Frequently Asked Questions

How do I collaborate with others for real-time coding?

Create New collab public project with a password and ask your friend to download HTML Editor PRO and go to collab coding option and enter your password in order to start coding in collaboration.

Can I host my websites and projects directly from the app?

Yes, HTML Editor PRO provides hosting functionality that allows you to host your websites and projects directly from the app. You can easily deploy your projects with just a few clicks.

How can I share or download my projects?

HTML Editor PRO offers easy project share/upload and download functionality. You can share your projects with others by generating shareable links or download them to your local machine for offline access.

Does the app provide syntax highlighting for code?

Yes, the HTML Editor PRO app includes syntax highlighting for enhanced code readability. It helps you easily identify different elements, attributes, and syntax elements in your code.
