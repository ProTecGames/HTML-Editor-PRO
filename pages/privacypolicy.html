Privacy Policy

Data Collection and Usage

We, ProTec Games, the administration of this app, value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. When you sign up and use this app, we collect and store certain data to provide you with the intended services.

The information collected includes your email address, which is only visible to us. We may use this email address to reach out to you for promotional purposes or to provide assistance related to the app.

Code Storage and GitHub Integration

When you upload code using our app, it is securely stored in a private GitHub repository owned and managed by us. The code is organized by user ID and filename and may be rendered and viewed through the app's interface. While the GitHub repository remains private, rendered versions of uploaded files (such as HTML) may be publicly viewable via browser previews using iframes.

By uploading code, you grant us permission to store and display it as intended by the app's functionality. We do not share your code with third parties outside the rendering and storage mechanisms described here.

Data Security

We take appropriate measures to ensure the security and confidentiality of your data. All user data, including uploaded files and coding collaboration, is stored securely either on our systems or on platforms we trust, like GitHub. Only authorized administration has access to this data.

Data Deletion

If you wish to delete your account and associated data from our app, including any uploaded code, please contact us using the provided support channels. We will promptly remove your data from our systems and associated repositories upon verification.

Data Analysis

We analyze data such as time spent on the app, actions performed, frequency of use, and ad interactions. This data is used solely to improve your experience and to guide future feature development.

Ads Preference

We serve ads using Google AdMob. Data related to ad interactions may be processed by AdMob during ad delivery. Please refer to their privacy policy for more details.

Email Preference

When you sign up for our app, you agree to be added to our Google Group for communication purposes. You may unsubscribe from emails at any time if you no longer wish to receive updates.

Payment Method

We currently accept payments via Google Play in-app purchases and PayPal. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for details on how they handle your payment information.

Changes to the Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to modify or update this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page. We encourage you to review this policy regularly.

If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy, please contact us.
